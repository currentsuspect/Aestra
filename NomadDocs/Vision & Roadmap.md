## Vision (preserved)

A fast, minimal, creator-first DAW that feels snappy on low-spec machines and scales up gracefully.

**Nomad's edge:**
- Pattern-first workflow optimized for Hip-Hop / electronic music production
- Extremely stable and intentional
- Fast hands UI
- Fewer workflows done exceptionally well

---

## Current Target: v1 Beta (December 2026)

The January 2026 roadmap reset established a realistic goal: **ship a credible, stable v1 Beta by December 2026**.

Key principles:
- **Stability first** — users must trust the app won't corrupt projects or drop audio
- **Scope ruthlessly** — one complete workflow beats ten half-finished features
- **Performance discipline** — measure, don't guess
- **Honesty** — if something can't ship reliably, it's out
